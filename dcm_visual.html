⚠️
The integration trap. DCM is the truth standard — but integrating it directly in onboard software causes slow orthogonality drift. Without periodic SVD re-orthogonalisation, RᵀR ≠ I and your attitude solution silently becomes physically invalid. This is why quaternions propagate the state, and DCM is used only for frame transforms.
